# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: January 2026**

## The Short Version

tidied does not collect any data. Period.

## What We Collect

Nothing.

- ❌ No personal information
- ❌ No usage analytics
- ❌ No crash reports
- ❌ No cookies or tracking
- ❌ No account required

## Where Your Data Goes

Nowhere. Your photos stay on your device. We have no servers.

## Photos Access

tidied requests access to your photo library solely to display photos for you to review. Photos you choose to delete are removed through Apple's Photos framework — we never upload, copy, or transmit your photos anywhere.

## Third-Party Services

None. We don't use any third-party SDKs, analytics, or services.
